How To Make Chocolate Zucchini Cake


  • Step 1

    Preheat oven at 180 C . Grease and dust your baking tray.

  • Step 2

    In a bowl, stir together flour, cocoa, salt, baking powder and soda bi carb.

  • Step 3

    In another bowl beat sugar and eggs until light and frothy.

  • Step 4

    Add the oil and vanilla essence.

  • Step 5

    Add this mixture to the dry ingredients also add the grated Zucchini and chocolate chips. Mix well.

  • Step 6

    Pour this batter into the baking tin , or two pans.

  • Step 7

    Bake for 45-50 minutes or till done.

  • Step 8

    Cool it for a while , cut into slices and serve!
